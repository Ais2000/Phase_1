About Us

Quench Cafe is a Filipino style cafe that serves simple, natural food – including Vegan and Gluten Free options – accompanied by authentic espresso coffee. Most coffee shops in the Philippines don’t do much with food and most restaurants don’t worry about coffee.

Our Coffee – We serve a range of espresso coffees made from the finest coffee beans, roasted right here in Cape Coral, and our baristas really know their beans. With their superior technical knowledge and a passion for making amazing coffee they will make you the perfect cuppa.

Our Food – Our food is all natural, simple food made with local ingredients, wherever possible, to provide you with fresher, healthier and deliciously tasty meals. We have a well stocked pastry cabinet, our food has an international flavor and we offer gluten free and vegan options.

Quench Cafe has a cool, trendy shabby chic decor and an upbeat fun atmosphere with a whole lot of colorful idioms: brekkie (breakfast), avo (avocado), and “no worries” (you’re welcome). Perfect place to relax on your own or to catch up with friends while enjoying greatness.
